ibooks highlights exporter

I see myself as a reader of books. I am a big reader. I have many books highlighted in iBooks. But shitty Apple doesn't give an automatic export function. I had to write it myself. Mostly I am too lazy to do this kind of thing, but this time... I am in some change-mode.... i building habit-streaks... long streaks. It all started with my morning run habit. i run everyday. then i read "The Power Of Habit" a really nice book about habits. How to build habits, how to replace them, how they work for individuals, organizations... the science behind habits, its mechanics... it had everything. So... i decided to write code everyday. EVERY SINGLE DAY. Now that's not easy. So... I thought I have to begin with something easy. This is the result. I'm pretty sure it will be useful to someone somewhere... anyway it'll be useful to me atleast. As always... who cares? :P
